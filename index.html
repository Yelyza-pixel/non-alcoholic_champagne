Who We Are

Founded by Artem Buniakov — oenologist, wine buyer, and luxury wine tourism expert — Éclat de Champagne is a brand that redefines how we celebrate, by merging finesse with a modern alcohol-free lifestyle. A sensory storyteller with experience in both traditional winemaking and innovation, Artem built Éclat to be as poetic as it is professional.

Our Vision

We envision Éclat as the centerpiece of contemporary celebrations — chic, elegant, and inclusive. We bring the same complexity and joy as fine Champagne, through delicate fermentation and refined craftsmanship, with none of the alcohol. Every bottle is a gentle revolution.

Celebrate With Style

From intimate dinners to grand celebrations, Éclat offers a sparkling touch that fits seamlessly into mindful lifestyles and elegant moments alike.

Modern Rituals

Éclat is not just a beverage — it’s a philosophy. Designed for the modern individual who values elegance without excess, taste without compromise, and rituals without risk.
